About Elytheria

Elytheria is a medieval idle RPG game where you play as a knight on a quest to save the kingdom from the evil dragon. You will face many challenges and battles, earn gold and experience, and upgrade your weapons and armor. Will you be able to defeat the dragon and restore peace to the land?
